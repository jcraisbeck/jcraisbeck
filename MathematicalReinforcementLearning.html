Mathematical Reinforcement Learning

My research topic is mathematical reinforcement learning, which at time of writing does not seem to have much of a definition, or a following. That may be because I have only affixed the word "mathematical" to the subject "reinforcement learning" as a way to placate my desire for rigor, but I do not think so. By mathematical reinforcement learning I mean an account of reinforcement learning which focuses on a formal understanding of the structures which already exist in Reinforcement Learning and in [discrete-time] decision processes.
